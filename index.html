About Me

Sasha Perigo is a writer, communications strategist, and affordable housing advocate from the Bay Area. She worked as a columnist at the San Francisco Examiner and has been published in Curbed SF, SF Weekly, Hoodline, and Mission Local. Most recently, she worked on strategic communications for mission-driven campaigns at East Bay Housing Organizations, Tenants Together, and The Worker Agency.
